## Title

Free Tesla Comparison Tool Helps Car Buyers Make Data-Driven Decisions

## Description

MyDreamTesla.com is a free online tool that helps prospective Tesla buyers compare every model, trim, and generation side by side. With over 100 vehicle configurations and 50+ specs per vehicle, it fills a gap that Tesla's own website doesn't address — cross-model and cross-generation comparisons.

Whether you're deciding between a Model 3 and Model Y, or comparing the original Model S against the 2022 Refresh, this tool puts all the data in one place: range, acceleration, pricing, cargo space, charging speeds, dimensions, and more.

Built as a side project by a developer who was frustrated by the lack of comprehensive Tesla comparison tools, MyDreamTesla.com requires no signup and is completely free to use.
